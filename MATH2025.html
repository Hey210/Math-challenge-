Math Challenge

How to Play:

Choose a difficulty level from the dropdown menu.

A math problem will appear. Enter your answer and click "Submit".

If correct, you earn a point; if not, 1 point is deducted.

Track your score displayed above.

Game Symbols Used:

*: Multiplication
-: Subtraction
/: Division
+: Addition

Known Bugs (FIXED) :

The game may not correctly handle negative numbers in subtraction problems.

Update Note: When answering subtraction, if you subtract a small number from a larger number, the result will be negative. example: 3 - 7 = -4 Ensure you account for this when entering your answers to avoid mistakes.
